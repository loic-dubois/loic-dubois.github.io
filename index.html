Loïc Dubois

ldubois@nd.edu

Postdoctoral researcher at the University of Notre Dame, under the supervision of Erin Chambers. I design and analyse algorithms for geometric and topological problems, with a focus on the drawings of graphs on surfaces. I defended my thesis on September 26, 2025; You may have a look at the manuscript, and the slides.

I used to be a phd student at Université Gustave Eiffel, advised by Éric Colin de Verdière and Vincent Despré. Before that, I studied at École Normale Supérieure de Lyon.
