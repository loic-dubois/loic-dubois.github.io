Loïc Dubois

ldubois@nd.edu

Postdoctoral researcher in Computational Geometry at the University of Notre Dame, under the supervision of Erin Wolf Chambers. I design and analyze efficient algorithms for geometric and topological problems. My focus is on representations of surfaces, and graphs drawn on surfaces. I defended my thesis on September 26, 2025; You may have a look at the manuscript, and the slides.

I used to be a PhD candidate at Université Gustave Eiffel, advised by Éric Colin de Verdière and Vincent Despré. Before that, I studied at École Normale Supérieure de Lyon.
