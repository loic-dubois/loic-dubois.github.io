Loïc Dubois

ldubois@nd.edu

I am a postdoc at University of Notre Dame, supervised by Erin Chambers. I design and analyse algorithms for geometric and topological problems, with a focus on graphs drawn on surfaces. I defended my PhD thesis on September 26, 2025. I used to be a phd student at Université Gustave Eiffel, supervised by Éric Colin de Verdière and Vincent Despré. Before that, I studied at École Normale Supérieure de Lyon.
